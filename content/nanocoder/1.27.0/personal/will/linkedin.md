🔥 Nanocoder 1.27.0 just shipped, and this one is a big deal.

The headline is Skills, a unified extension surface that finally brings custom commands, subagents, and tools under one roof. Drop a directory under `.nanocoder/skills/<name>/` with a `skill.yaml` manifest and everything fans out into the existing registries automatically. No more patchwork.

There's also a new background daemon (file-watching and cron subscriptions, running headlessly without prompts). Custom Tools (markdown-defined, model-callable), LLM-based context compaction, config lint at startup, two OOM fixes, and a new Nanocoder Battlemap comparing us against Claude Code, Codex CLI, Gemini CLI, Aider, and others across twelve axes.

We're building this in the open, Nanocoder is community-built, open source, and put together for the collective rather than for profit.

Very excited for where this is heading.
