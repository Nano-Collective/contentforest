We've launched the Nano Collective sponsorship program.

If you've been using any of our tools - Nanocoder, Nanotune, get-md, json-up - and want to support the work, here's how.

Sponsorship is recurring. Three productised tiers: Individual ($5, $25, or $100/month), Supporter ($250/month), Builder ($1,000/month). There's also a bespoke door for larger or custom arrangements. Every detail - tiers, perks, placement - is live at https://nanocollective.org/sponsor.

Support doesn't have to be cash, either. We're equally open to non-cash sponsorship: cloud and API credits, compute, software licenses, hardware, and other resources that help contributors build.

Funds flow through Open Source Collective. Every transaction is on the public ledger in real time. No hidden cuts, no management fees, no profit-taking. The money either covers the cost of running the collective (infrastructure, hosting) or goes to the community fund, which pays bounties to open-source contributors. The Economics Charter has the exact mechanism documented if you want the full picture.

We're independent. No investors. No profit motive. Everything stays open.

The sponsor wall is empty at launch. That's intentional. Early sponsors help set the tone for everyone who comes after. If the model works for you, your company, or your team, the door is open: https://nanocollective.org/sponsor
