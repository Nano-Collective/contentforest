The Nano Collective sponsorship program is live.

I started the collective because I believe building open-source software is worth sustaining, and I didn't want corporate funding dictating the direction.

Now there's a way to support the work directly. Three sponsorship tiers - from $5/month all the way up - and every dollar flows transparently through Open Source Collective, straight to the community fund that pays contributors. No hidden fees.

It doesn't have to be cash, either. We also welcome credits, compute, and other resources that help the work.

If you've been using Nanocoder, Nanotune, get-md, or json-up, consider becoming a sponsor. The details are on the website.
