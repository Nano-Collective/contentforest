The Nano Collective has launched its sponsorship program. The program funds the community fund that pays bounties to open-source contributors.

Sponsorship tiers are productised: Individual ($5, $25, or $100/month), Supporter ($250/month), or Builder ($1,000/month). There is also a bespoke partnership door for larger or custom arrangements. Full details: tier benefits, placement, and the full pitch, live at https://nanocollective.org/sponsor.

Support does not have to be cash. We also welcome non-cash sponsorship such as cloud and API credits, compute, software licenses, and hardware. In-kind support is recognised on the same basis as financial support.

Funds flow through Open Source Collective. Every inflow and outflow is visible on the public ledger in real time. There are no hidden cuts, no management fees, and no profit-taking: the model is exactly as documented in the collective's Economics Charter.

The four shipped projects: Nanocoder, Nanotune, get-md, and json-up, are independent, community-led work. No investors. No profit motive. Everything stays open.

The sponsor wall is empty at launch. That's intentional: early sponsors set the tone. If the model works for you, your company, or your team, the door is at https://nanocollective.org/sponsor.
